āvaraṇaparicchedo dvitīyaḥvyāpi prādeśikodriktasamādānavivarjanam|dvayāvaraṇamākhyātaṃ navadhā
kleśalakṣaṇam

saṃyojanānyāvaraṇamudvegasamupekṣayoḥ|tattvadṛṣṭeśca satkāyadṛṣṭestadvastuno'pi ca

nirodhamārgaratneṣu lābhasatkāra eva ca|saṅkleśasya parijñāne śubhādau daśadhā'param

aprayogo'nāyatane'yogavihitaśca yaḥ|notpattiramanaskāraḥ sambhārasyā'prapūrṇatā

gotramitrasya vaidhuryaṃ cittasya parikheditā|pratipatteśca vaidhuryaṃ kuduṣṭajanavāsatā

dauṣṭhulyamavaśiṣṭatvaṃ trayāt prajñā'vipakvatā|prakṛtyā caiva dauṣṭhulyaṃ kausīdyaṃ ca pramāditā

saktirbhave ca bhoge ca līnacittatvameva ca|aśraddhā'nadhimuktiśca yathārutavicāraṇā

saddharme'gauravaṃ lābhe gurutā'kṛpatā tathā|śrutavyasanamalpatvaṃ samādhyaparikarmitā

śubhaṃ bodhiḥ samādānaṃ dhīmattvā'bhrāntyanāvṛtī|nṛtyatrāso'matsaritvaṃ vaśitvañca śubhādayaḥ

trīṇi trīṇi ca eteṣāṃ jñeyānyāvaraṇāni hi|pakṣyapāramitābhūmiṣvanyadāvaraṇaṃ punaḥ

vastvakauśalakausīdyaṃ samādherdvayahīnatā|aropaṇā'tha daurbalyaṃ dṛṣṭidauṣṭhulyaduṣṭatā

aiśvaryasyā'tha sugateḥ sattvā'tyāgasya cāvṛtiḥ|hānivṛddhyośca doṣāṇāṃ guṇānāmavatāraṇe

vimocane'kṣayatve ca nairantarye śubhasya ca|niyatīkaraṇe dharmasambhogaparipācane

sarvatragārthe agrārthe niṣyandāgrārtha eva ca|niṣparigrahatārtha ca santānā'bheda eva ca

nissaṅkleśaviśuddhyarthe'nānātvārtha eva ca|ahīnā'nadhikārthe ca caturdhāvaśitāśraye

dharmadhātāvavidyeyamakliṣṭā daśadhāvṛtiḥ|daśabhūmivipakṣeṇa pratipakṣāstu bhūmayaḥ

kleśāvaraṇamākhyātaṃ jñeyāvaraṇameva ca|sarvāṇyāvaraṇānīha yatkṣayānmuktiriṣyate

||ityāvaraṇaparicchedo dvitīyaḥ||
