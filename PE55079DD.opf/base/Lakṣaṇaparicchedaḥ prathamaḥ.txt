ārya maitreyapraṇītāmadhyāntavibhāgakārikālakṣaṇaparicchedaḥ prathamaḥlakṣaṇaṃ hyāvṛtistattvaṃ
pratipakṣasya bhāvanā|tatrā'vasthā phalaprāptiryānā ''nuttaryameva ca

abhūtaparikalpo'sti dvayaṃ tatra na vidyate|śūnyatā vidyate tvatra tasyāmapi sa vidyate

na śūnyaṃ nā'pi cā'śūnyaṃ tasmāt sarvaṃ vidhīyate|sattvādasattvāt sattvācca madhyamā pratipacca
sā

arthasattvātmavijñaptipratibhāsaṃ prajāyate|vijñānaṃ nāsti cāsyārthastadabhāvāttadapyasat

abhūtaparikalpatvaṃ siddhamasya bhavatyataḥ|na tathā sarvathā'bhāvāt tatkṣayānmuktiriṣyate

kalpitaḥ paratantraśca pariniṣpanna eva ca|arthādabhūtakalpācca dvayā'bhāvācca deśitaḥ

upalabdhiṃ samāśritya nopalabdhiḥ prajāyate|nopalabdhiṃ samāśritya nopalabdhiḥ prajāyate

upalabdhestataḥ siddhā nopalabdhisvabhāvatā|tasmācca samatā jñeyā nopalambhopalambhayoḥ

abhūtaparikalpaśca cittacaittāstridhātukāḥ|tatrārthadṛṣṭirvijñānaṃ tadviśeṣe tu caitasāḥ

evaṃ pratyayavijñānaṃ dvitīyaṃ caupabhogikam|upabhogaparicchedaprerakāstatra caitasāḥ

chādanādropaṇāccaiva nayanātsaṃparigrahāt|pūraṇāt triparicchedādupabhogācca karṣaṇāt

nibandhanādābhimakhyād duḥkhanāt kliśyate jagat|tredhā dvedhā ca saṃkleśaḥ saptadhā'bhūtakalpanāt

lakṣaṇaṃ cā'tha paryāyastadartho bheda eva ca|sādhanañceti vijñeyaṃ śūnyatāyāḥ samāsataḥ

dvayā'bhāvo hyabhāvasya bhāvaḥ śūnyasya lakṣaṇam|na bhāvo nā'pi cā'bhāvao na pṛthaktvaikalakṣaṇam

tathatā bhūtakoṭiścā'nimittaṃ paramārthatā|dharmadhātuśca paryāyāḥ śūnyatāyāḥ samāsataḥ

ananyathā'viparyāsatannirodhāryagocaraiḥ|hetutvāccāryadharmaṇāṃ paryāyārtho yathākramam

saṃkliṣṭā ca viśuddhā ca samalā nirmalā ca sā|abdhātukanakākāśaśuddhivacchuddhiriṣyate

bhoktṛbhojanataddhehapratiṣṭhāvastuśūnyatā|tacca yena yathā dṛṣṭaṃ yadarthaṃ tasya śūnyatā

śubhadvayasya prāptyartha sadā sattvahitāya ca|saṃsārā'tyajanārthaṃ ca kuśalasyā'kṣayāya ca

gotrasya ca viśuddhayarthaṃ lakṣaṇavyañjanāptaye|śuddhaye bu[ddha]dharmāṇāṃ bodhisattvaḥ prapadyate

pudgalasyā'tha dharmāṇāmabhāvaḥ śūnyatā'tra hi|tadabhāvasya sadbhāvastasmin sā śūnyatā'parā

saṃkliṣṭā cedbhavennāsau muktāssyuḥ sarvadehinaḥ|viśuddhā cedbhavennā'sau vyāyāmo niṣphalo bhavet

na kliṣṭā nā'pi va'ākliṣṭā śuddhā'śuddhā na caiva sā|prabhāsvaratvāccittasya kleśasyāgantukatvataḥ

||iti lakṣaṇaparicchedaḥ prathamaḥ||
