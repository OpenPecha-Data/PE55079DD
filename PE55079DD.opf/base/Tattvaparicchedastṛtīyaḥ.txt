tattvaparicchedastṛtīyaḥmūlalakṣaṇatattvamaviparyāsalakṣaṇam|phalahetumayaṃ tattvaṃ sūkṣmaudārikameva
ca

prasiddhaṃ śuddhiviṣayaṃ saṅgrāhyaṃ bhedalakṣaṇam|kauśalyatattvaṃ daśadhā ātmadṛṣṭivipakṣataḥ

svabhāvastrividho'sacca nityaṃ saccā'pyatattvataḥ|sadasattattvataśceti svabhāvatrayamiṣyate

samāropā'pavādasya dharmapudgalayoriha|grāhyagrāhakayoścāpi bhāvā'bhāve ca darśanam

yajjñānānna pravarteta taddhi tattvasya lakṣaṇam|asadartho hyanityārtha utpādadavyayalakṣaṇaḥ

samalā'malabhāvena mūlatattve yathākramam|duḥkhamādānalakṣmākhyaṃ sambandhenā'paraṃ matam

abhāvaścāpyatadbhāvaḥ prakṛtiḥ śūnyatā matā|alakṣaṇañca nairātmyaṃ tad-vilakṣaṇameva ca

svalakṣaṇañca nirdiṣṭaṃ duḥkhasatyamato matam|vāsanā'tha samutthānamavisaṃyoga eva ca

svabhāvadvayanotpattirmalaśāntidvayaṃ matam|parijñāyāṃ prahāṇe ca prāptisākṣātkṛtāvayam

mārgasatyaṃ samākhyātaṃ prajñaptipratipattitaḥ|tathodbhāvanayaudāraṃ paramārthantu ekataḥ

arthaprāptiprapattyā hi paramārthastridhā mataḥ|nirvikārā'viparyāsapariniṣpattito dvayam

lokaprasiddhamekasmāt trayād yuktiprasiddhakam|viśuddhagocaraṃ dvedhā ekasmādeva kīrtitam

nimittasya vikalpasya nāmnaśca dvayasaṅgrahaḥ|samyagjñānasatattvasya ekenaiva ca saṅgrahaḥ

pravṛttitattvaṃ dvividhaṃ sanniveśakupannatā|ekaṃ lakṣaṇavijñaptiśuddhisamyakprapannatā

ekahetutvabhoktṛtvakartṛtvavaśavartane|ādhipatyārthanityatve kleśaśuddhyāśraye'pi ca

yogitvā'muktamuktatve ātmadarśanameṣu hi|parikalpavikalpārthadharmatārthena teṣu te

anekatvā'bhisaṅkṣepaparicchedārtha āditaḥ|grāhakagrāhyatadgrāhabījārthaścā'paro mataḥ

veditārthaparicchedabhogāyadvārato'param|punarhetuphalāyāsānāropā'napavādataḥ

aniṣṭeṣṭaviśuddhīnāṃ samotpattyādhipatyayoḥ|samprāptisamudācārapāratantryārthato'param

grahaṇasthānasandhānabhogaśuddhidvayārthataḥ|phalahetūpayogārthanopayogāttathā'param

vedanāsanimittārthatannimittaprapattitaḥ|tacchamapratipakṣārthayogādaparamiṣyate

guṇadoṣā'vikalpena jñānena parataḥ svayam|niryāṇādaparaṃ jñeyaṃ saprajñaptisahetukāt

nimittāt praśamāt sārthāt paścimaṃ samudāhṛtam||||iti tattvaparicchedastṛtīyaḥ||
